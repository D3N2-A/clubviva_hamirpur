## Bauhaus — Neo-Brutalist

## North Star: "Form Follows Function"
Bold, raw, and unapologetic. Inspired by Bauhaus and brutalist architecture. High contrast, strong geometry, deliberate imperfection.

---

## 1. Visual Language & Principles

### 1.1 Core Aesthetic
- **High Contrast:** Black and white foundations with single, high-saturation accent colors.
- **Strong Geometry:** Heavy use of rectangles, thick borders (2px-4px), and 90-degree angles.
- **Typography as Image:** Text isn't just for reading; it's a structural element. Large, bold headings that define the layout.
- **Deliberate Rawness:** Unfiltered, "default" browser aesthetics refined through professional spacing and alignment.

### 1.2 Layout Strategy
- **The Grid is King:** Visible or implied borders that segment the page into clear, functional blocks.
- **Asymmetry:** Balanced but non-traditional placement of elements to create visual tension and interest.
- **Negative Space:** Generous "white space" (often black or grey in this theme) to let bold elements breathe.

---

## 2. Design Tokens

### 2.1 Color Palette
- **Primary:** `#1a1a1a` (The Deep Base)
- **Secondary:** `#FFFFFF` (The Clean Surface)
- **Accent:** `#F2CA50` (The Golden Action - used for CTAs and highlights)
- **Border:** `#333333` (The Structural Guide)

### 2.2 Typography
- **Headings:** `Space Grotesk` — A idiosyncratic sans-serif with quirky details that perfectly captures the neo-brutalist spirit.
- **Body:** `Inter` or `Space Grotesk` (Light) — Optimized for high legibility at smaller scales.
- **Hierarchy:** Extreme scale shifts (e.g., 80px headlines next to 14px labels).

### 2.3 Shape & Elevation
- **Roundness:** `4px` (Minimal/Standard) or `0px` (Hard Edge).
- **Shadows:** Hard "Pop" shadows (e.g., `4px 4px 0px #000000`) instead of soft blurs.

---

## 3. Component Guidelines

- **Buttons:** Rectangular, high-contrast, often with a thick black border. Hover states should feel "mechanical" (color swaps, position shifts).
- **Cards:** Defined by borders rather than shadows. No rounded corners beyond the 4px token.
